Latitude vs Humidity


Sampled cities closer to the equator appear to be more humid, as one might guess. The scatter shows a pretty significant drop off in humidity as you move away from the equator in both directions. This is not to say that cities sampled at more latitudinal extremes did not measure instances of 100% humidity, but the variance between cities at these extremes is greater than their equatorial counterparts. Anomalously, there is a cluster of cities between 60 and 75 latitude with 0% humidity. The sampled data can make no conclusive arguments about the cluster, but additional reasearch into these cities might prove interesting.
