Latitude vs Cloudiness


Cloudiness appears to not significantly relate to latitude. The included scatter seems to suggest that cloudy extremes are more common than partially cloudy weather. Sampled cities at 40 or greater latitude appeared to experience more cloudy weather, as one might expect. However, this may be due to the sample biasing towards norther cities. More data is required to reach any difinitive conclusions.
