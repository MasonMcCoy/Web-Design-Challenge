Latitude vs Max Temperature


To the surprise of no one, maximum temperature increases as you approach the equator. It should be noted, however, that there are a handful of cities with significantly higher max temperatures than we might expect. There are two cities, for example, that share max temperatures with other cities that exist at or near the equator, despite being located between 50 and 60 latitude. Also anomalous is the cluster of cities that have the highest max temperature of the sample, even though they range widely from -20 to -40 latitude. The trend, however, is still apparent: cities experience higher maximum temperatures as they approach the equator.
